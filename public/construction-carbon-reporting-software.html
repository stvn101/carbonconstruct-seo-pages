The problem with existing tools

Every carbon tool was built by someone who's never held a nail gun

Built for consultants, not builders

Enterprise tools assume a dedicated sustainability manager. Most mid-tier builders have a PM wearing four hats. The tool needs to work for them.

Disconnected from the workflow

Carbon is calculated at the end of the project, not during procurement. By then you can't change anything. The data is useless for the next job.
